Privacy Policy

Bhagavad Gita Quotes - Hindi is a privacy-friendly Chrome extension. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s local storage only to save:

  • Favorite quotes
  • Last viewed quote
  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)

All data remains on your device and is never sent anywhere.

Permissions

  • storage – to save favorites and preferences locally
  • notifications – to show daily Bhagavad Gita quotes
  • alarms – to schedule daily notifications

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party APIs.
